Have a sweet tooth? French macarons, opera cake, mille-feuille, éclairs, and croissants are just a few of the mouthwatering pastries offered in Paris. Parisian cafés serve as the center of social and culinary life in Paris, and the perfect spot to relax, undisturbed, for a few hours.

Dress up for a night of entertainment at the famous cabaret. Once known for showing its Cancan dances, the hall now offers a glamourous show of singing and dancing that you won’t forget!

Discover a unique perspective of the city as you glide under Paris’s many bridges and past the Notre Dame Cathedral, Eiffel Tower, and more breathtaking views. It is the perfect atmosphere for both a romantic evening or a fun day with friends!
